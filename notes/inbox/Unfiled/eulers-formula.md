# Euler's Formula

1. $$ e^{i\pi} + 1 = 0 $$

1. $$ e = \lim_{n\to\infty} \left(1 + \frac{1}{n}\right)^n  = \lim_{n\to\infty}\frac{n}{\sqrt[n]{n!}} $$

1. $$ e = \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} \frac{1}{n!} $$
